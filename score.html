A Note From Muzz:


For those who are new to the scoring it's quite simple.

1 - Find the link relevant for the day you're playing.

2 - Find the sheet with your name on it. Each player has a sheet with their name on it. DO NOT CHANGE THE NAME of any sheets.

3 - After each hole enter your score (off the stick) into the score column (skins and stableford points are all worked out in the back-end so no need to worry about that). If you have a wipe put 9. You must enter a score for every hole or Wishnu will strike you down.
